Design Principles

Visual Hierarchy

Good design guides the user's eye seamlessly through the content. By establishing a clear visual hierarchy, we ensure that the most critical information stands out immediately.

  1. Contrast improves readability.
  2. Alignment creates structure.
  3. Repetition builds consistency.

Accessibility Matters

Ensure your contrast ratios meet WCAG AA and AAA standards. Learn more at our Accessibility Guide.
